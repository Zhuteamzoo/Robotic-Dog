# Planetary Gearbox

Since I'm making a QDD motor I will be using a planetary gearbox. The whole idea of QDD motors is a low reduction planetary gearbox with a high torque BLDC pancake style motor. I hear that there is a lot of math related with this and that seems interesting.
I've also seen Aaed Musa's planetary gearbox and it seems different from the others I've seen during my research.
