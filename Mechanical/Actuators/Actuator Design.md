# Actuator Design

After the numerous sites that I have consulted through the other files in this folder, I have decided to use a Quasi Direct Drive (QDD) actuator for my robotic dog. I will use a pancake BLDC motor with a planetary gear box with a relatively 
low gear ratio like around 9:1. I will trying to aim for ~16Nm of torque. I feel like this would be enough to accomplish my goals of walking and dancing.
